About the Project

This is a linguistic database that documents and organizes the phonological, grammatical, and lexical features of languages across Eurasia.

It is designed to support comparative and historical research by allowing users to explore similarities and differences among languages spoken from Western Europe through Central Asia to East Asia.

The database provides structured, searchable information that helps researchers analyze patterns of linguistic diversity, language contact, and regional typology across the Eurasian continent.
